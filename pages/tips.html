6 Simple Ways to Help Our Planet

1. Plant Trees 🌳
         Trees give us oxygen and reduce pollution.
2. Save Water 💧
         Use water carefully. Don’t waste it while brushing or washing.
3. Use Less Plastic 🧴
         Carry reusable bags and bottles instead of plastic ones.
4. Recycle ♻️
         Recycle paper, glass, and metal to reduce waste.
5. Save Electricity 💡
         Turn off lights and fans when not in use.
6. Keep Your Surroundings Clean 🧹
         Dispose of waste properly to keep your area neat and healthy.
🌏 Bonus Tip!

Try using public transport or bicycles instead of cars to reduce air pollution.
